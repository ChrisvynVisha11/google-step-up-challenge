# Google Step Up Challenge — Gemini Pro Launch Strategy

Data-driven $10M marketing strategy to drive Gemini Pro 
student sign-ups across 4 markets.

## Analysis Performed
- Analysed 1,600+ weeks of historical campaign data 
  and 48 brand lift studies
- Applied Z-testing (p < 0.05) to validate campaigns
- Calculated CPA and Cost Per Lifted User (CPLU) 
  across channels and markets
- Clustered markets by efficiency

## Key Findings
- High-Efficiency cluster (SA/EG) delivers 4x more 
  sign-ups per $1 vs European markets
- Search delivers lowest CPA globally at $7.69
- "Life Hack" creative drove +12% higher consideration
- Recommended $6.5M to High-Efficiency markets

## Tools
Python, pandas, NumPy, statsmodels, Z-testing
